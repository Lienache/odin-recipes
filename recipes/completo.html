How to make a real completo

A "Completo italiano" is a traditional and the most famous chilean fastfood

Want to make ur own completo?

You will need:

  • 4 Completo's buns
  • 4 Sausages
  • 2 Avocados
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • Mayo
  • Salt

Step by step

How to do a Completo Italiano?

  1. Prepare the avocado: Cut and mash 2 avocados in a bowl with 1 tsp. of salt. If you want a creamier texture, add 1 tbsp. of olive oil.

  2. Chop the tomato: Wash, peel, and dice the 2 tomatoes into small cubes. Then, place them in a bowl, add ½ tsp. of salt, and mix well.

  3. Cook the sausages:Boil water in a small saucepan and, when hot, add the 4 sausages. Cook for 3-4 minutes, until piping hot. You can also put them in a frying pan if you prefer a crispier texture.

  4. Heat the buns:Open the buns and heat them in the oven for 3 minutes at 180°C, or in a pan without oil until warm.

  5. Lets assemble:Put a sausage inside each bun. Then, add chopped tomato, then a generous layer of avocado, and finish with mayonnaise to taste.

  6. Serve and enjoy:Pair your Completo Italiano with a cold drink or fresh juice. And don't forget the napkins!
